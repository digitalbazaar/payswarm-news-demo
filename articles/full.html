PaySwarm for Content Creators

The PaySwarm standard has a number of features that are beneficial to content creators:

  • Easy to deploy into Drupal, Wordpress, and other content systems.
  • Much more secure and safe than credit-cards
  • Simple customer experience.
  • Flexible payments from $0.00001 to thousands of dollars.
  • Based on open standards like HTTP, TLS, and OAuth.
  • PaySwarm itself is an open, patent and royalty-free standard.

This site is a demonstration of the PaySwarm technology. You won't be charged anything to use this site. It is provided merely to demonstrate the customer experience that is achievable via PaySwarm.

You must have a valid PaySwarm account via a PaySwarm Provider. A PaySwarm account is much like a bank account. You deposit money into it and approve charges to it when you purchase content on the Web, much like you use a debit card. Since PaySwarm is an open standard, you choose your PaySwarm Provider and any PaySwarm-compliant website will work with your provider.

The Benefits

Using PaySwarm benefits Content Creators in a number of different ways, some of which are outlined in the following sections.

Easy Deployment

Deploying PaySwarm into existing Content Management Systems (CMS) like Drupal, Wordpress or Joomla is easy. PaySwarm purchasing and digital contract retrieval can be done using standard OAuth libraries in under a hundred lines of code. Because PaySwarm is built on top of the OAuth standard, which is used by Twitter, Google, Netflix, and Yahoo, there are many libraries in many different languages to choose from when it comes to implementing PaySwarm.

Secure Financial Transactions

PaySwarm is built around the concept of a Payment Token, which provides much stronger financial protections than a credit card or debit card when used online. A Payment Token is like a debit card that is specifically assigned to a content provider's website. The Payment Token has a spending limit that is set by the customer before being activated. The Payment Token is then assigned to a specific website and can only be used by that website until the spending limit has been met. Customers are protected by excessive fraudulent spending by the spending limit. Content creators are protected against excessive damages due to theft of the Payment Token because of the spending limit and because Payment Tokens can always be revoked at any time by the customer or content website.

In most cases, stealing a customer's credit card number could result in hundreds of dollars in damages. Using PaySwarm, stealing a customer's per-website Payment Token may result in damages up to $1. However, whoever steals the customer's Payment Token would only be able to transfer money into the content creator's financial that owns the Payment Token.

Simple Customer Experience

PaySwarm does not require any extra browser software for customers and will therefore work in any modern web browser. PaySwarm is fully-integrated into your website, which means that you can show your customer's how much is left of their spending limit, what they've purchased previously, and a variety of other information about their spending on your website. The only time a customer is redirected to another website is to authorize the Payment Token. The content creator stays in control of the experience at all other times.

Flexible Payments

The payments that are allowed using a PaySwarm Payment Token can range from thousands of dollars, to pennies, to thousandths of a a penny. This enables content creators to charge per year and per book down to per second and per article. PaySwarm can be used for macropayments as well as micropayments, and allows content creators to determine how much or how little to charge their customers.

Open and Standards-based

PaySwarm is built on top of open or standard Web technologies. There are no black-boxes or overly-restrictive digital rights management systems involved. All that is required for PaySwarm to operate is HTTP, TLS, RDFa and JSON-LD. This ensures full interoperability between different implementations. If you'd like to help develop the PaySwarm standard, you are urged to join the community.

Royalty-free

Anyone can implement and use the PaySwarm standard in a patent and royalty-free manner. You don't need permission, you don't need to pay royalties.
